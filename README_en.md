## üìñ Overview

PCs, the Internet, mobile, cloud. Technology has always been mastered by the young, who drove innovation forward. Senior generations, trapped by past successes and ingrained biases, could not embrace new technologies.

With generative AI, that structure has reversed for the first time.

The quality of AI output depends on the depth of experience, knowledge, and context of the person using it. For the first time in the history of technology, the accumulation of experience directly translates into technological advantage ‚Äî a singularity.

Yet the senior generation ‚Äî the greatest beneficiaries ‚Äî is not passing on the structure of that advantage to the next generation.

Humanity has evolved by transferring knowledge from the experienced to the young. This is a civilizational argument against the breaking of that chain in the age of AI.

This book arms the young with a "Thinking OS," confronts seniors with their responsibility,<br>and proposes a new intergenerational contract for the AI era.

## üîë Core Framework

### 10:80:10 ‚Äî The Thinking OS

The 10:80:10 framework, originally proposed as an efficiency model for new business development, is redefined in this book as "the mental OS for remaining human in the AI era."

| Phase     | Original Definition       | Redefinition in This Book                                           |
| --------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| First 10% | Problem setting           | **Your will** ‚Äî What to ask, what to seek. Your axis                |
| 80%       | AI processing             | AI output ‚Äî This can be delegated                                   |
| Last 10%  | Finishing the deliverable | **Judgment** ‚Äî Evaluating whether AI's output aligns with your axis |

### Critical Thinking ‚Äî A Technology to Protect Yourself

Not a skill for efficiency. A defense technology for maintaining your own axis against AI output.

### The Theory-Practice Loop

Theory can be learned from this book. But theory alone does not become capability. Test theory in the field of practice, then feed that experience back into theory. Only this loop builds essential competence.
